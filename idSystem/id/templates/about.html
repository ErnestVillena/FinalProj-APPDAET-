ABOUT THE APP

This app is designed to help users manage their identification documents in a secure and convenient manner. It allows users to store various types of IDs, such as government-issued IDs, student IDs, and more, all in one place.

Users can easily add, view, and delete their IDs, ensuring that they have quick access to their important documents whenever needed.
